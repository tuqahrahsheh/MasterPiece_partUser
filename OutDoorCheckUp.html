Outdoor Checkup Service

Our Outdoor Checkup service brings healthcare professionals right to your doorstep, providing personalized medical consultations and health assessments in the comfort of your own home. We ensure convenience, quality, and professional care for you and your loved ones.

With a team of certified doctors and nurses, we are dedicated to making healthcare accessible and stress-free. From routine checkups to wellness evaluations, your health is our priority.

Our Outdoor Checkup Services

Home Visit Consultation: Receive personalized medical advice from a qualified doctor in the comfort of your home.

Routine Health Checkup: Comprehensive health assessments to ensure your overall well-being.

Elderly Care: Specialized medical care and follow-up services for the elderly.

Post-Surgery Follow-Up: Medical visits to monitor your recovery after surgery.

Pediatric Care: Health services for children, including vaccinations and health checks.

Chronic Disease Management: Ongoing medical monitoring and care for chronic conditions.
